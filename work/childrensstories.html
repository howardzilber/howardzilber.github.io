Children's Stories

I have a kid. I’ve worked with kids. And if my memory is accurate, I used to be a kid myself. So it’s no surprise that I love children’s stories. Still, it was a surprise to me when I woke up one morning with an idea for a kid’s book about a poor tailor who had nothing, but was still expected to make a regal robe for the king. Since that time several other such stories have come to me and have been written down. They fall into two groups. There are the stories that just came. Then there are the stories that derive from my experience as a father. Let’s call them the Zoey stories. One of those is a group of stories that hangs together and became a bigger story.
